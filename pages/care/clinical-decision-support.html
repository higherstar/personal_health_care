Description

Increased volumes of healthcare data are now available to both HCPs and patients1,2 This overwhelming growth in healthcare data is making clinical decisions more complex. High-quality Clinical Decision Support (CDS) leverages objective health data and analytics to improve individual patient outcomes by enabling more confident clinical decisions in real time at the point of care.
