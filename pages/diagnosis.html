ADVANCED IMAGEING

Description

Advanced imaging is a type of Meaningful Data at Scale. It refers to the use of computer algorithms to analyze digital outputs of X-rays, computed tomography (CT), ultrasound, magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) and positron emission tomography (PET) that are used for medical diagnosis.
