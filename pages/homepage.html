THE PERSONALISED HEALTHCARE (PHC) SYSTEMS MAP

The complexity of PHC calls for us to be systems thinkers with the ability to think and act from a whole system perspective and make decisions in light of the long term health of the whole system. This map will provide a broad picture view and exploration of components of the PHC ecosystem, the interconnectedness of the system at play, and how you can engage with PHC.
